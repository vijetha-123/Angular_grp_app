Kanopi & Partners

There's strength in numbers.

Kanopi partners with some of the best, and we believe these partnerships make our work and community stronger. It’s why, “community” is one of our values.

We have existing partnerships with hosting providers including Pantheon(Drupal and WordPress), Acquia(Drupal), WP Engine(WordPress), and Platform (Drupal). Through these partnerships, we are able to recommend the best hosting service for your individual needs.

We are members of the Drupal Association, and have several staff members who are Acquia certified.

We also use Lingotek for multilingual needs and translation services, and SiteImprove so we can check the accessibility of your site.
